The Strategy.

Upgrading aesthetics, performance, and user experience to drive measurable business growth.

Higher Conversion.

A streamlined 3-step inline form allows visitors to request information instantly. By removing unnecessary steps, we dramatically increase the likelihood that interested visitors complete the process.

Modernized Brand.

A clean, premium aesthetic that reflects the quality of the Treasure Tower program. We can optionally tie the hero theme directly to your direct mail campaigns for a cohesive experience.

The Post-Submission Experience.

Exactly what happens the moment a prospective owner takes action.

1. Instant Engagement

Upon submitting the request info form, the prospect is seamlessly shown a high-quality introductory video, capitalizing on their peak interest.

2. Automated Nurture

Simultaneously, a branded welcome email is dispatched. This provides additional resources and keeps the opportunity top-of-mind.

3. Actionable Lead

The data is securely logged into your WordPress / CRM ecosystem. Your team is alerted with a rich lead profile, ready for a warm follow-up.

Execution Timeline.

A structured approach to launch within a fast two-week sprint.

Week 1

Strategy & Design

  • • Designing the request info form, page content, and overall layout while exploring variations.
  • • Creating full-color page designs that seamlessly align with your brand and marketing.
  • • Presenting two distinct layout and design concepts for your review and approval.
Week 1-2

Development

  • • Developing in a secure, local staging environment to ensure zero disruption to the live site.
  • • Building out the form and all page elements so the experience feels incredibly smooth.
  • • Ensuring pixel-perfect execution on mobile devices.
Week 2

Wrapping Up Development

  • • Adding safety checks to prevent fake phone numbers or zip codes.
  • • Connecting the form directly to the existing WordPress system and/or CRM.
  • • Implementing partial data capture to follow up with leads who start but drop off.
  • • Conducting initial testing of data routing and form functionality.
Week 2 End

Final Testing & Go-Live

  • • Final rigorous cross-browser and cross-device testing to ensure pixel-perfect presentation.
  • • Optionally setting up custom tracking links for tracking postcard leads.
  • • Final review and official launch of the new pages.
  • • Commencement of 30-day post-launch support for tweaks, adjustments, and fixes.
