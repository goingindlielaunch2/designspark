The Big Idea

The 4-Step Action Plan

Targeted solutions to make the website easier to use, build trust, and gather contact information from interested buyers.

Step 1: The First Impression

Fixing the Message

The Issue: Currently the website tries to talk to everyone at once, causing confusion.

The Solution: Update the top banner to clearly route the user based on your primary goals.

Visual Example: The New Top Banner

Step 2: Simplifying the Menu

Making Navigation Easy

The Issue: The top menu has too many overlapping options (Request Info, Request Owner Info, etc.) which confuses visitors.

The Solution: Create a clean, simple menu with one highly visible "Action Button" (like "Get Free Info Kit") that stays on the screen even as the user scrolls down.

Visual Example: The Always-Visible Button

Logo
ConceptFAQ
Free Info Kit

This menu stays glued to the top of the screen as they scroll.

Step 3: Showcasing Success Stories

Making Reviews Irresistible

The Issue: The video reviews from happy owners are your best selling tool, but they are currently just thumbnails with the owners' names. Give the users more reason to view them.

The Solution: Include an exciting quote next to the video from the owner to give visitors a reason to hit "Play".

Visual Example: The Video Quote

"I replaced my full-time income!"

- Tomeka Hoff

Step 4: Gathering Interested Contacts

Offering a "Free Gift"

The Issue: Asking people to "Request Info" can feel scary because they worry a salesperson will call them immediately.

The Solution: Instead of asking them to talk to us, we offer them something valuable for free (like a PDF "Business Blueprint") in exchange for their email address.

How it works:
  • • User enters just their First Name and Email.
  • • The website immediately takes them to a "Thank You" page.
  • • An automated email instantly sends them their Free Info Kit.

The Customer Journey

How a casual visitor naturally becomes a highly interested buyer.

1

Instant Connection

The user lands on the website. The top banner immediately speaks to their specific desires, routing them to the correct information instantly.

2

Easy Education

They scroll down and read a beautifully designed list of benefits (Low Overhead, Free Training, Cash Flow) instead of heavy paragraphs.

3

Watching Success Stories

They reach the "Success Stories" section, read an exciting quote next to a video, click it, and watch the review right there on the page.

4

A Small, Easy Step

After the video ends, they see a friendly prompt right below it: "Ready to see the numbers? Download our Free Info Kit."

Thank You & Video Delivery

They enter their email and are instantly taken to a "Thank You" page featuring a friendly welcome video from your team.

Inbox Follow-Up

An automatic email delivers their Info Kit. They are now an interested contact in your system, ready to be followed up with!
