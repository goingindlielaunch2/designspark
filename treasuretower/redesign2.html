What Happens After They Sign Up.

Exactly what happens the moment a potential owner fills out the form.

1. Immediate Connection

As soon as they submit the form, the visitor is smoothly shown a high-quality introductory video while they are most interested.

2. Automatic Follow-Up

At the same time, a welcoming email with your branding is sent. This gives them more helpful resources and keeps your business fresh in their minds.

3. Ready-to-Call Contact

Their information is securely saved into your website's database. Your team gets an alert with all their details, ready for a friendly follow-up call.

Project Timeline.

A clear step-by-step plan to get your new site live in just two weeks.

Week 1

Planning & Design

  • • Designing the form, the text on the page, and how everything is arranged.
  • • Creating page designs that perfectly match your business colors and style.
  • • Showing you two different design options to pick from.
Week 1-2

Building the Site

  • • Building the new pages on a private hidden link so your current website stays exactly as it is until we are ready.
  • • Putting together the form and all the pieces of the page so everything works smoothly.
  • • Making sure everything looks perfect and is easy to tap on mobile phones.
Week 2

Finishing the Build

  • • Adding safety checks to prevent fake phone numbers or zip codes.
  • • Connecting the form straight to your current website's database.
  • • Setting up a way to save info from people who start filling out the form but don't finish it.
  • • Testing the form to make sure emails and details go to the right place.
Week 2 End

Final Checks & Launch

  • • Final testing on different phones, tablets, and web browsers (like Chrome and Safari) to make sure it looks great everywhere.
  • • Optionally setting up special web links so you know which contacts came from your postcards.
  • • Final review and official launch of the new pages.
  • • Starting your 30 days of free support after launch for any small changes or fixes.
