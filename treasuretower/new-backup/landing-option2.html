How exactly does the Treasure Tower work?

+
The Treasure Tower is an 8-slot prize dispenser that uses tokens to reward kids. You place them in locations like dental offices or schools, and service the route by restocking and collecting revenue on a schedule that fits your life.

Do I need to pay monthly franchise fees?

+
No. Unlike many other opportunities, Treasure Tower has no monthly royalties or ongoing franchise fees. Once you own your equipment, you keep your profits.
