Simplifying the Property Assessment Workflow

The Challenge: Field adjusters were using a fragmented, legacy system to input property data, resulting in high cognitive load, data entry errors, and prolonged estimation times.

The Solution: Led the end-to-end UX overhaul of a cross-platform (tablet/desktop) data entry tool. Partnered with R&D and engineering to map complex data states. Re-architected the information flow into a logical, phased wizard that reduced time-on-task by 35% and drastically improved data accuracy.

  • Conducted solo generative research with 15 field operators.
  • Navigated strict technical constraints of offline-first mobile architecture.

Building a Scalable Enterprise Component Library

The Challenge: Rapid product expansion led to design inconsistencies across 4 core applications. Engineering teams were duplicating effort, and UX reviews were bottlenecked by visual QA.

The Solution: Championed and built a centralized, reusable UX component library in Figma using atomic design principles. Advocated for design tokens and collaborated closely with front-end engineering to ensure 1:1 parity between Figma components and React code.

  • Created robust Figma variants, auto-layout rules, and interactive prototypes.
  • Acted as a role model, mentoring 2 junior designers on system consumption.

How I Deliver Value

My process is rooted in deep user empathy, cross-functional collaboration, and strategic execution—aligning directly with the needs of high-impact product teams.

End-to-End Execution

From ambiguous problem statements to performant UI delivery. I excel at mapping complex information architectures and translating them into clear, actionable wireframes and high-fidelity designs.

Cross-Functional Advocacy

I partner closely with Product, R&D, and Engineering. I understand the capabilities and limitations of web/mobile tech stacks, ensuring my designs are not just memorable, but buildable.

Data-Driven Empathy

Design without research is just decoration. I utilize both qualitative user insights and quantitative product data to invent solutions, validate assumptions, and drive clarity with senior leadership.

Beyond the Pixels

I believe that great UX is a team sport. While I take pride in my craft—mastering tools like Figma, creating complex user flows, and detailing UI interactions—my true strength lies in communication.

I strive to be a role model within design teams, advocating for agile methodologies and design thinking. Whether I'm mentoring a junior designer, scoping a project with a PM, or reviewing feasibility with engineering, my goal is to foster a culture of quality and innovation.
