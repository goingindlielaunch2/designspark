Timeline & Constraints

1

Empathize

Objective: Uncover user needs and friction points using high-speed AI data mining, followed by human validation.

Action Steps:

  • AI Mine public discussions, subreddits, or forums to cluster emotional themes.
  • Human Validate AI themes with real user interviews.
  • Human Run bias checks to ensure no demographic or experiential skew.

Required Deliverables

  • AI Affinity Map vs. Human Synthesis: Raw AI clusters side-by-side with refined insights.
  • The Business Context: Summary of project necessity (e.g., "Mobile drop-offs cost $5k/mo").
2

Define

Objective: Define exact problems to solve and create rigorous, testable user models.

Action Steps:

  • AI Generate 3 OCEAN-calibrated personas for multi-turn testing.
  • Human Condense research into a single, portfolio-ready executive summary.

Required Deliverables

  • Problem Statement (Portfolio Hook): [Target User] needs a way to [Action] because [Insight], but currently faces [Obstacle].
  • Success Metrics (The "Before"): Establishing baseline metrics (e.g., "4 min task time").
3

Ideate

Objective: Rapidly generate, evaluate, and narrow solutions using UX principles.

Action Steps:

  • AI Divergence: Generate 30+ wild ideas and extreme edge cases.
  • Human Convergence: Narrow to viable options based on technical feasibility.
  • Human Heuristic Audits: Run ideas against NN/g 10 Usability Heuristics.

Required Deliverables

  • Design Decisions & Trade-offs: Documentation of what was discarded and why.
  • Heuristic Evaluation Report: Breakdown of violations mapped to NN/g heuristics.
4

Prototype

Objective: Build testable solutions and pressure-test designs against simulated stakeholders.

Action Steps:

  • AI Variant Generation: Rapidly generate 4-5 UI variants for selection.
  • AI Stakeholder Simulation: Dry-run pushback (e.g., "Act as a skeptical Tech Lead").
  • Human Develop Prototype: Build the interactive high-fidelity flow.

The Portfolio Proof Format

Prompt: What you asked the AI.
AI Output: The raw draft or variant.
Human Decision: Logic for modification/rejection.
5

Test

Objective: Cycle through simulated testing, age the design, and validate with real humans.

Action Steps:

  • AI Synthetic Testing: Catch surface-level issues via AI personas.
  • AI "Age" the Design: Prompt AI to simulate 3 weeks of daily usage.
  • Human Real-User Gate: Mandatory live usability tests to confirm accuracy.

Required Deliverables

Before & After Visuals:

High-contrast comparisons annotated with problems solved.

Impact Metrics (The "After"):

Hard metrics (conversion %) and Soft metrics (confidence scores).

6

Retrospective

Objective: Demonstrate self-awareness, growth, and senior-level product ownership.

Action Steps:

  • Human Review lifecycle for bottlenecks and personal growth areas.
