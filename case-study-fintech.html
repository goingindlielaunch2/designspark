The Challenge

A complete overhaul of a legacy banking interface, focusing on accessibility and data visualization transparency for over 50k active users. The goal was to simplify complex financial data into actionable insights.

The Solution

We implemented a modular dashboard design with customizable widgets. The color palette was carefully chosen to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards while maintaining a modern, dark-mode-first aesthetic.
