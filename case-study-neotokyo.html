Interaction Design

Neo-Tokyo Shop

Role

Creative Developer

Timeline

4 Weeks

Tech

WebGL, Three.js

The Concept

An immersive shopping experience for a high-end streetwear brand. The client wanted to move away from traditional grids and creating a feeling of exploring a digital city.

Execution

We implemented WebGL product views and micro-interactions that respond to cursor movement. The result is a fluid, game-like shopping journey that increased session duration by 200%.
