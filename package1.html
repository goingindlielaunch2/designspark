Plain English Glossary

We use a few technical terms in this proposal. Here is exactly what they mean for your bottom line.

Conversion

When a visitor takes an action you want — like filling out a form or calling you. More conversions = more customers.

Above-the-Fold (The Hero Section)

What people see immediately when the site loads, before they scroll. It's critical because visitors decide to stay or leave in seconds. In this proposal, we focus heavily on the Hero Section as it is your digital first impression.

HERO SECTION

Call-to-Action (CTA)

A button or prompt (like 'Request Info' or 'Get Started') that encourages visitors to take the next step.

User Experience (UX)

How easy and clear the website is to use. Good UX makes it effortless for visitors to understand you and reach out.

Conversion-Optimized

A design built specifically to turn visitors into customers using proven layouts, messaging, and visual psychology.
