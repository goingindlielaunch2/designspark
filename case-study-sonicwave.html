Visualizing Sound

An experimental app for visualizing distorted sound mixes in real-time. The project was a study in combining haptic feedback with fluid, gesture-based navigation controls.

Interaction

Users can manipulate audio waves directly through touch. We used distinctive neon gradients and glitch effects to represent different sound textures.
